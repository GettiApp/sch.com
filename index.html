Save Melon Sandbox!

Melon Sandbox, formerly known as Melon Playground, has been unjustly removed from the Google Play Store due to a false copyright claim.

This creative sandbox game allows players to experiment with physics and build unique contraptions. The removal is a blow to the game's developers and the gaming community.

We urge you to help raise awareness about this issue! Share this website and use the hashtag #SaveMelonSandbox on social media.

Let's work together to get Melon Sandbox back on the Google Play Store!
